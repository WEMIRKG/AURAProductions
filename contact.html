FAQ

How long does it take to get my commercial?

Quick social ads are usually delivered within 48 hours once we have your payment and project details. Larger cinematic promos typically take 5–7 days.

Do you offer refunds?

Because every video is custom-made, refunds are generally not offered after production starts. We do, however, include revisions with each package and work with you to reach a result you’re proud of.

Can you work with any niche or industry?

In most cases, yes. We review each project to ensure it aligns with our creative standards and content policies. If we aren’t a fit, we’ll let you know and may suggest alternatives.

How do payments work?

You can pay via Venmo and PayPal using the details on our Pricing page. Once payment and your intake form are submitted, we lock in your slot and begin production.
